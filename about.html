Hello! My name is Lizbeth Ramirez and I am a graduating senior at Michigan Technological University. I will be graduating with my B.A. in Scientific and Technical Communication with an emphasis in Business Communication and a certificate in Digital Media. I am an avid concert goer, who loves smiling, cooking, and stand up paddle boarding, I also write and design.

then

I was born and raised in Lancaster California. Here, I was surrounded by my family and friends and was always a short drive to Los Angeles. I saw the sun 280 days out of the year and I was in love with the beach. In hightschool, I dual enrolled at my community college and was able to receive an Associates degree in Liberal Arts and Sciences the day I earned my highschool diploma. In the summer of 2012, I visited Michigan Tech's campus for the Leading Scholars competition and the rest is history.

now

I am currently a graduating senior at Michign Technological University. Despite the fact that I had no family or friends in Michigan, not to mention the fact that I until then, I had only seen snow twice in my life, I knew when I arrived that I had found home. I majored in Scientific and Technical Communication and will also be recieving my certifiate in Digital Media. My next step in life wil take me to Kohler, WI where I have accepted a full-time position as a Technical Writer.
